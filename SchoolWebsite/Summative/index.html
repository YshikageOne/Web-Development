Welcome to the City of Davao

Davao City is the largest city in the Mindanao region of the Philippines and the third-most populous city in the country. It is a highly urbanized city in the Davao Region and serves as the regional center and the largest city in the region.

City Hall

Davao City Hall is the seat of the local government of Davao City, located at San Pedro Street, Poblacion District, Davao City, Philippines.

Waling-waling

Waling-waling (Vanda sanderiana) is a species of orchid that is endemic to the Philippines. It is considered one of the most beautiful orchids in the world and is often referred to as the "Queen of Philippine Orchids".
